# Sub Portal Overview

The Sub Portal is your dedicated workspace to view work, submit bids, manage documents, and track payments from general contractors who use ContractorOS.

---

## What You Can Do

| Task | How the Portal Helps |
|------|---------------------|
| See your work | View assigned projects and schedules |
| Submit bids | Respond to bid requests |
| Stay compliant | Upload insurance and licenses |
| Get paid | Submit invoices and track payments |
| Communicate | Message project teams |

---

## Getting Started

### Receiving an Invitation

When a GC adds you to ContractorOS:

1. You receive an email invitation
2. Click the link to access portal
3. Create your password
4. Complete your profile
5. You're ready to work

### First Login

After creating your account:

1. **Complete your profile** — Company info, contacts
2. **Upload documents** — Insurance, licenses, W-9
3. **Set notification preferences** — How you want to be contacted
4. **Add team members** — If applicable

## Viewing Your Work

### Active Projects

See all projects you're assigned to:

1. Click **My Projects**
2. View list of active projects
3. Click any project for details

### Project Details

For each project, access:
- Your scope of work
- Drawings and specifications
- Schedule and dates
- Team contacts
- Your payment status

### Documents & Drawings

View project documents:
1. Open the project
2. Go to **Documents**
3. Download what you need
4. Always have latest versions

---

## Bid Requests

### Viewing Bid Requests

When a GC requests a bid:

1. You receive an email notification
2. Bid request appears in your dashboard
3. Click to view details

### Bid Request Details

Each request includes:
- Project name and location
- Scope of work
- Drawings and specs (attachments)
- Bid due date
- GC contact information

### Submitting a Bid

1. Review the scope carefully
2. Click **Submit Bid**
3. Enter your price
4. Add estimated duration
5. Include any notes/clarifications
6. Attach supporting documents (optional)
7. Submit

### Bid Status

Track your bids:
- **Submitted** — Waiting for response
- **Under Review** — GC is evaluating
- **Awarded** — You won the work
- **Not Selected** — Another sub was chosen

## Compliance Documents

### Why Documents Matter

GCs need your documents to:
- Protect against liability
- Meet insurance requirements
- Verify your credentials
- Comply with regulations

### Required Documents

Typically required:
- Certificate of Insurance (COI)
- Workers' Compensation certificate
- General Liability certificate
- Business license
- W-9 form

### Uploading Documents

1. Go to **My Profile → Documents**
2. Click **Upload**
3. Select document type
4. Choose file
5. Set expiration date (if applicable)
6. Save

### Expiration Tracking

The system tracks expirations:
- Dashboard shows upcoming expirations
- Reminders sent before expiration
- May block new work if expired
- Easy to update with new documents

## Invoicing

### Submitting an Invoice

1. Go to **Invoices**
2. Click **Submit Invoice**
3. Select the project
4. Enter invoice details:
   - Invoice number
   - Amount
   - Description of work
   - Period covered
5. Attach your invoice PDF
6. Submit

### Invoice Status

Track your invoices:
- **Submitted** — Waiting for review
- **Under Review** — GC is processing
- **Approved** — Ready for payment
- **Paid** — Payment sent
- **Returned** — Needs changes

### Viewing Payment History

1. Go to **Payments**
2. See all payment history
3. Filter by date, project, status
4. Download payment records

## Common Questions

### How do I update my insurance?

Go to Profile → Documents, click on the expired document, and upload the new one.

### Can I see what other subs are bidding?

No. Bids are confidential. You only see your own submissions.

### How long until I get paid?

Payment timing depends on the GC's terms. Check your agreement or ask them directly.

### What if I can't complete work on schedule?

Contact the project team immediately through the portal. Early communication prevents problems.

### Can multiple people from my company access the portal?

<!-- STATUS: COMING_SOON -->
Multi-user access is coming soon. For now, one login per company.

### How do I know if I won a bid?

You'll receive a notification and the bid status will update to "Awarded."

## Tips for Success

### Respond Quickly

- Bid requests have deadlines
- GCs appreciate fast responses
- Set up notifications so you don't miss requests

### Keep Documents Current

- Upload new documents before expiration
- Avoid being blocked from bidding
- Shows professionalism

### Invoice Promptly

- Submit invoices as soon as work is complete
- Include all required information
- Faster submission = faster payment

### Communicate Clearly

- Ask questions early
- Report issues immediately
- Document everything through the portal
