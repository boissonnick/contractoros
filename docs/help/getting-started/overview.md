# Welcome to ContractorOS

ContractorOS is a complete construction management platform built for general contractors, remodelers, and specialty trades. Whether you're managing multi-million dollar commercial projects or residential renovations, ContractorOS keeps your team, clients, and subs on the same page.

## What Can You Do With ContractorOS?

### For Owners & Project Managers

| Job to Be Done | How ContractorOS Helps |
|----------------|------------------------|
| Win more work | Create professional estimates and proposals with e-signature |
| Keep projects on track | Visual schedules, task management, and real-time updates |
| Get paid faster | Generate invoices, track payments, integrate with accounting |
| Manage your team | Assign work, track time, run payroll |
| Reduce liability | Document everything with photos, daily logs, and change orders |
| Delight clients | Give clients their own portal to see progress and approve decisions |

### For Field Workers

| Job to Be Done | How ContractorOS Helps |
|----------------|------------------------|
| Know what to do today | See your schedule and tasks on your phone |
| Log your time easily | One-tap clock in/out, or use voice logs |
| Document your work | Take photos, add notes, mark tasks complete |
| Report issues fast | Flag problems immediately so they get resolved |

### For Clients

| Job to Be Done | How ContractorOS Helps |
|----------------|------------------------|
| See project progress | Real-time updates, photos, and schedule |
| Make decisions | Approve change orders, select finishes |
| Communicate easily | Message your contractor, see responses |
| Stay informed | Automated updates, no phone tag required |

### For Subcontractors

| Job to Be Done | How ContractorOS Helps |
|----------------|------------------------|
| Get clear scope | See exactly what's expected, with drawings and specs |
| Submit bids | Respond to bid requests with your pricing |
| Track your money | See what you've invoiced and what's been paid |
| Coordinate schedules | Know when you're needed on site |

## Your First 15 Minutes

After you sign up, here's what to do first:

### Step 1: Complete Your Profile (2 min)
Your profile helps your team and clients know who you are.

1. Click your name in the bottom-left corner
2. Add your photo (optional but recommended)
3. Verify your phone number for notifications
4. Set your notification preferences

→ [Complete guide: Setting up your profile](/help/settings/profile.guide.md)

### Step 2: Set Up Your Organization (5 min)
*For Owners/Admins only*

Customize ContractorOS for your company:

1. Go to **Settings → Organization**
2. Add your company logo
3. Set your brand colors (used in client-facing documents)
4. Add your company address and contact info
5. Configure your default settings

→ [Complete guide: Organization settings](/help/settings/organization.guide.md)

### Step 3: Invite Your Team (3 min)
*For Owners/Admins only*

Get your team on the platform:

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email and select their role
4. They'll receive an email invitation

→ [Complete guide: Managing your team](/help/team/invite-members.guide.md)

### Step 4: Create Your First Project (5 min)

Now you're ready to manage work:

1. Click **+ New Project** in the sidebar
2. Enter the project name and address
3. Add client information
4. Set the project type and dates

→ [Complete guide: Creating a project](/help/projects/create-project.guide.md)

## Understanding the Interface

### The Sidebar
Your main navigation. Access all modules from here:
- **Dashboard** - Your command center
- **Projects** - All your jobs
- **Clients** - Your customer database
- **Schedule** - Calendar and assignments
- **Team** - Your people
- **Reports** - Insights and analytics
- **Settings** - Configuration

### The Header
- **Search** - Find anything (projects, clients, tasks) instantly
- **Notifications** - Stay updated on what needs attention
- **Help** - Access June (AI assistant) and documentation
- **Profile** - Your account and preferences

### Context-Sensitive Actions
On every page, look for:
- **+ buttons** - Create new items
- **⋮ menus** - More actions for existing items
- **Filter/Sort controls** - Narrow down what you see

## Getting Help

### Ask June
June is your AI assistant. Click the **?** icon in the bottom-right corner to:
- Ask questions in plain English
- Get help with any task
- Find features you're looking for
- Troubleshoot issues

Example questions for June:
- "How do I create a change order?"
- "Where can I see my team's time entries?"
- "What's the status of the Johnson project?"

### Browse Documentation
You're reading it! Use the sidebar to explore help articles by topic.

### Contact Support
For issues June can't resolve:
- Email: support@contractoros.com
- In-app: **Help → Contact Support**
