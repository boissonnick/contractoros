## 5. Key Success Factors

### Product Requirements

1. **Mobile-first design** — 93% smartphone usage on-site
2. **Offline capability** — Many job sites lack reliable connectivity
3. **Simple onboarding** — 70% have no tech roadmap
4. **Affordable pricing** — SMBs allocate 1-5% of revenue to tech
5. **Quick time-to-value** — ROI expectations under 24 months

### Go-to-Market Considerations

1. **Target residential first** — Smaller deal sizes but higher volume
2. **Focus on pain points** — Scheduling, client communication, change orders
3. **Geographic concentration** — Texas, Florida, California for initial traction
4. **Freemium/trial model** — Lower barrier to entry for skeptical adopters
5. **Integration focus** — QuickBooks, accounting tools are table stakes
