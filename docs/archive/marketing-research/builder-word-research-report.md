## Strong Runner-Ups

### TEKTON
- **Meaning:** Ancient Greek for "builder, craftsman" (root of "architect")
- **Problem:** Crowded space - Tecton.ai, Tekton.dev, Tektonic.ai all exist
- **Verdict:** Great word, too much competition

### OPUS
- **Meaning:** Latin for "work, masterpiece"
- **Problem:** Claude Opus, OpusClip, opus.ai - heavily saturated
- **Verdict:** Beautiful word, completely taken

### WRIGHT
- **Meaning:** Old English for "maker" (shipwright, playwright)
- **Problem:** wrightai.com taken by active AI company
- **Verdict:** Classic but unavailable

### FECIT
- **Meaning:** Latin for "he/she made it" (artist signature)
- **Problem:** fecit.ai already operating (AI dubbing)
- **Verdict:** Elegant but taken
